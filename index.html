A digital storyteller who loves to turn words and experiences into pictures and videos. During covid lockdowns, I started making little videos for people to celebrate birthdays, anniversaries, surviving lockdowns, and more. It brought people little moments of happiness and reassured them they weren’t alone, and I realized I could create things that make people feel.

As a student in the University of Waterloo’s Global Business and Digital Arts program, I have explored a variety of mediums, from long-form YouTube videos and stylized photoshoots to short-form social media content and fast-paced sport photography. Known for creating short films to document memories and celebrate milestones, my work often focuses on connecting events of the past with a certain emotion to create nostalgic, joyful, or bittersweet narratives.

When I’m not creating, I can be found figure skating or crocheting. I'm also not one to turn down a good book or movie as I'm always looking for inspiration.

If you love stories as much as I do, or are looking for someone who can tell yours, connect with me on LinkedIn and let’s create!
